About Me

Hello World! I am Rob Fenger, born july 9 1993 in Haarlem, the Netherlands. I studied philosophy at the university of Amsterdam, where I got my bachelor and master. Furthermore, I have a grade for teaching at highschool-level. But, as you can see, some things changed and now I changed course during the Coronaperiod and I started to learn how to code at Codecademy.com, where I finished the course Web Development.

Although the study of philosophy is known as an alpha-course, I am naturally more bèta-oriented. At my highschool I was enrolled in math B and D (which is a course for the math-nerds). While I was studying philosophy I was tutoring math for highschool- and MBO/HBO-students. Also there is within the study philosophy a course Logic, which is based on structural thinking. This is to give structure to ones thinking and writing, and has proven very helpfull in learning to code.

In my free time I like to read and write. Obviously I like to read the works of the great philosophers, but also novels once in a while. My writings consist of poetry and spoken word and musically I'm into rap music, which I write and record as well. Furthermore, I play soccer and like to cook some good and healthy foods.
